Welcome!

Hello, I am an artist born in Chennai, India, brought up in Dubai, U.A.E. I'm am currently in my 4th year persuing a Fine Arts Major & Digital Arts Minor in the California Institute of the Arts. I mostly come from a background in mixed media art & experimental animation, but also indulge in other indiscplianry fields I like. Some of them are music, cooking, poetry, philosophy, film & visual art of different forms. I like to meld different ideas & disparate fields of art & ideas to create something intriguing & new. My art website can be viewed by clicking on the hyperlink.

My professional practice is Motion Design & Branding and I'm currently working on building my skills & portfolio. I like to work on Title Squences for music videos & films and am trying to persue a career within that field.

I also have a band called Royal Tandoor where I mostly play guitar & sing. Some of the genres we cover are indie, ragtime, spoken word, vaudville, country & others. We sometimes like to approach our music almost as 'role-playing' different charecters/perspectices & like to have a general experiental approach. We are currently working on some recordings which which will have around 70-80 tracks (a triple EP of sorts) and will be released in December after mixing & production. Some musicians I also like and am inspired by are the Beach Boys, A.R Rahman, Wilco, Kanye West, Guided by Voices, Indian music, etc.
